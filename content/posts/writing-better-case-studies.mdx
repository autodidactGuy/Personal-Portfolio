A good case study does not just say what was built. It explains:

- the problem context
- the system constraints
- the decisions that mattered
- the measurable outcome

## A simple structure

Start with the business problem, then move into technical constraints, implementation choices, and results. That sequence helps technical and non-technical readers stay aligned.
